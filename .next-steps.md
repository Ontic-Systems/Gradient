### 3. Comptime Metaprogramming ⭐ FOUNDATIONAL
**Rationale:** Research brief calls this "the clear winner for LLM-first" - replaces macros, generics, conditional compilation

**What's Needed:**

- Integration with current generics system

**Example from Research:**
```gradient
fn Vector(comptime T: type, comptime N: usize) -> type:

```

**Blocker for:** Self-hosting compiler
